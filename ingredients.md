Ingredients:
    2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

    Lettuce leaves
    Tomato slices
    Mayonnaise or your preferred sandwich spread
    Cheese slices (optional, like cheddar or Swiss)
    1 tablespoon butter (for toasting the bread)
